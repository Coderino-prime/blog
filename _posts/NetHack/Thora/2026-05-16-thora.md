## Thora...
...a dwarvish valkyrie wielding the artifact hammer of her namesake God - Mjollnir - and the King's Sword - Excalibur. She ventured bravely into the depths of Gehennom and retrieved the Amulet from the dark priest of Moloch. Wearing the Jarngreipr and a helm of dwarvish iron, with scales of a gray dragon and mirror shield, she valorantly carried the amulet out of the depths of the Mazes and bestowed the amulet rightfully to Tyr.
